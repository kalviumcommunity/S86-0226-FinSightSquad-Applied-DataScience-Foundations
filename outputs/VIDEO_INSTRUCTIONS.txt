Video recording checklist (approx. 2 minutes)

1. Open your project workspace in the file explorer or VS Code.
2. Show `data/raw/` and explain: "Raw = original, never modified."
3. Show `data/processed/` and explain naming conventions for processed files.
4. Show `outputs/` (figures/reports) and describe what goes where.
5. Mention that scripts read from `data/raw/` and write to `data/processed/`/`outputs/` only.
6. State where you uploaded the video and paste the link into `outputs/video_link.txt`.

Tips: Keep the camera/screen visible, speak clearly, and keep it to ~2 minutes.
