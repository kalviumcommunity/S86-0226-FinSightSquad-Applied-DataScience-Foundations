KEY CONCEPTS TO EXPLAIN
------------------------

1. Boxplot Components:
   - Box = middle 50% of data (Q1 to Q3)
   - Line in box = median (50th percentile)
   - Whiskers = extent to min/max (within 1.5 × IQR)
   - Dots = outliers beyond whiskers

2. IQR (Interquartile Range):
   - Calculated as Q3 - Q1
   - Represents spread of middle 50%
   - Used to identify outliers

3. Outliers:
   - Points beyond 1.5 × IQR from Q1 or Q3
   - Not always errors (need investigation)
   - May represent unusual but valid values

4. Why Boxplots Matter:
   - Quick distribution summary
   - Easy multi-group comparison
   - Clear outlier identification
   - Shows both center and spread
